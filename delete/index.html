Instructions for End Users and Partners

How to Request Deletion

Please follow the instructions based on your relationship with us.

For End Users of Partner Applications

If you use an application built on the Fraia platform (e.g., an app from PeoplesDoctor, Nordisk Film, etc.), you are an "End User".

As stated in our Privacy Policy, the company that provides the application to you is the Data Controller. You must contact that company directly to request the deletion of your account and data.

We cannot process deletion requests that come directly from End Users, as we must act on the instructions of our Partners (the Data Controllers).

For Our Direct Partners

If you are a direct business customer ("Partner") of Fraia and wish to delete your Partner account and all associated data from our platform, please send an email to:

sadeq@fraia.ai

Please use the subject line: "Partner Account Deletion Request". We will process your request in accordance with our service agreement.
