Our Role as a Data Processor

Understanding our role is key to understanding this policy. Our platform is a business-to-business (B2B) service.

This policy applies to the AI application platform ("Platform") and related services provided by [Your Company Name] ("Fraia", "we", "us", "our").

Our business customers ("Partners") use our Platform to build and deploy their own AI applications for their customers ("End Users"). In this relationship:

  • Our Partner is the Data Controller. They decide what End User data to collect and how it is used within the applications they build on our Platform. They are responsible for legal compliance regarding that data.
  • Fraia is the Data Processor. We process End User data only on the documented instructions of our Partner, as outlined in our agreement with them.

If you are an End User of an application provided by one of our Partners, please direct your privacy-related questions to that Partner.

Information We Process

We process two categories of information: data on behalf of our Partners, and data we collect for our own purposes.

1. Data Processed on Behalf of Our Partners

The type of End User data we process is determined by our Partners and the applications they create. This may include, but is not limited to:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, user ID, phone number.
  • User-Generated Content: Text from conversations with AI, documents uploaded, queries submitted.
  • Vision Data: Images or videos uploaded for analysis by AI vision models.
  • Any other data our Partners choose to collect through their applications.

2. Data We Collect Directly

We collect information necessary to manage our relationship with our Partners:

  • Partner Account Information: Contact details, billing information, and communication records for our Partners' representatives.
  • Platform Usage Data: Aggregated, anonymized data about how our Partners use the Platform to monitor performance, improve our services, and ensure security.
